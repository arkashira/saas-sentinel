# Customer Journey Map for SaaS-Sentinel

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: SaaS product teams notice declining user engagement metrics.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of visibility into user behavior and context; existing analytics tools are too generic.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, concern about user retention, urgency to find a solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide educational content on the importance of user context; offer free webinars or workshops on improving engagement.
- **Metric**: Number of impressions on marketing materials, website visits, and sign-ups for webinars.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Product teams begin researching solutions to improve user engagement.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of available tools; difficulty in comparing features and benefits.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, skepticism about the effectiveness of new tools, cautious optimism.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer detailed comparison guides, case studies showcasing success stories, and personalized demos.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and engagement with comparison materials.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Teams sign up for a free trial of SaaS-Sentinel.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; lack of immediate insights; potential technical issues during integration.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; hope for quick results.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a seamless onboarding experience with guided setup; offer responsive customer support during the trial period.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rate and time to first meaningful insight.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Teams see positive results from using SaaS-Sentinel during the trial.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about pricing and long-term value; potential resistance from stakeholders.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction with initial results but apprehension about commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Present tailored pricing plans based on usage; highlight ROI through success metrics; provide testimonials from similar companies.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Teams recognize the value of SaaS-Sentinel and consider additional features or user licenses.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about which additional features will provide the most value; budget constraints.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the product but cautious about expanding investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized consultations to identify additional needs; provide incentives for expanding usage (e.g., discounts for multi-user licenses).
- **Metric**: Upsell conversion rate and average revenue per user (ARPU).
